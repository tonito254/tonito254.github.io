Origins of life in Kibwezi

LifeinKibwezi is a website created by Muiva and Mulinge, aimed at capturing and documenting the diverse and beautiful town of Kibwezi. The website showcases the gradual growth of Kibwezi through generations, as seen through the lens of skilled photographers. Our goal is to highlight the unique characteristics and attributes of Kibwezi, from its natural beauty to its rich culture and history. We hope that our collection of photographs will not only be visually stunning, but also serve as a tribute to the people and places that make Kibwezi so special.

The founders

Lifeinkibwezi is a community-based organization founded by Edward Mulinge, a dedicated social entrepreneur and community leader with a passion for improving the lives of people in Kibwezi town and its surrounding areas. Edward is a native of Kibwezi, and his deep roots in the community have inspired his lifelong commitment to creating positive change through education, healthcare, and sustainable development. With over 20 years of experience in community organizing and advocacy, Edward has worked tirelessly to promote social justice and economic empowerment for all members of the Kibwezi community. He has collaborated with local leaders, government officials, and international organizations to develop and implement a wide range of initiatives, including educational programs, health clinics, vocational training, and entrepreneurship projects. Through his leadership of Lifeinkibwezi, Edward has created a dynamic platform for community engagement and action, bringing together people from all walks of life to work towards a shared vision of a more just, prosperous, and sustainable future for Kibwezi and its people. With his deep commitment to community service and his innovative approach to social change, Edward Mulinge is a true pioneer and a driving force behind the growth and development of Kibwezi town and its surrounding areas.

Tony Muiva is a multi-talented individual who wears many hats as the founder, engineer, and photographer of LifeinKibwezi. Tony's passion for capturing and documenting the beauty of Kibwezi and its people has been the driving force behind the creation of LifeinKibwezi. As an engineer, Tony has worked on several projects aimed at improving the lives of the people of Kibwezi. His commitment to community development has led him to focus on creating sustainable solutions that address the unique challenges faced by the people of Kibwezi. Tony's passion for photography has enabled him to capture the essence of Kibwezi and its people. Through his lens, he has documented the diverse culture, stunning landscapes, and vibrant community of Kibwezi. His photographs have been featured in several exhibitions and have gained recognition both locally and internationally. At LifeinKibwezi, Tony brings together his engineering expertise and his passion for photography to create a platform that celebrates the beauty and diversity of Kibwezi. Through LifeinKibwezi, Tony hopes to inspire and empower the people of Kibwezi by showcasing their strengths, resilience, and rich cultural heritage.
